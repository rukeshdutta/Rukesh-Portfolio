Customer segmentation with RFM

Customer segmentation using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) is a powerful technique to divide customers into distinct groups based on their buying behavior. It enables businesses to identify their most valuable customers, understand their needs, and tailor marketing strategies accordingly. RFM analysis can help increase customer retention, loyalty, and revenue.

Customer cohorts analysis

The customer cohorts analysis is a powerful tool for evaluating campaign performance. By grouping customers into segments based on shared characteristics and behavior, it enables businesses to identify trends and patterns that can inform their marketing strategies. This approach allows companies to better understand the lifetime value of their customers and optimize their campaigns for maximum impact.

Recommendation system

A grocery recommendation system using collaborative filtering in Python can provide personalized recommendations to customers based on their purchase history and preferences. By leveraging an item matrix, the system can suggest similar products to customers based on their past purchases or the purchases of similar customers. This can improve customer engagement and sales for grocery retailers.
